# References

## Data resources

1. The Cancer Genome Atlas Breast Invasive Carcinoma cohort, TCGA-BRCA.  
   Used as the primary RNA-seq training, validation, and testing cohort.

2. UCSC Xena TCGA data hub.  
   Used to access TCGA-BRCA expression data and clinical/PAM50-related metadata.

3. cBioPortal Breast Cancer METABRIC study: Breast Cancer (METABRIC, Nature 2012 & Nat Commun 2016).  
   Used as the source for METABRIC external validation data.

## Breast cancer molecular subtype biology

4. The Cancer Genome Atlas Network. Comprehensive molecular portraits of human breast tumours. Nature, 2012.  
   Relevant for breast cancer molecular subtypes and integrated breast cancer molecular characterization.

5. Perou CM, Sørlie T, Eisen MB, et al. Molecular portraits of human breast tumours. Nature, 2000.  
   Foundational paper for intrinsic breast cancer molecular subtypes.

6. Parker JS, Mullins M, Cheang MCU, et al. Supervised risk predictor of breast cancer based on intrinsic subtypes. Journal of Clinical Oncology, 2009.  
   PAM50 subtype classifier reference.

## METABRIC

7. Curtis C, Shah SP, Chin SF, et al. The genomic and transcriptomic architecture of 2,000 breast tumours reveals novel subgroups. Nature, 2012.  
   Core METABRIC study.

8. Pereira B, Chin SF, Rueda OM, et al. The somatic mutation profiles of 2,433 breast cancers refine their genomic and transcriptomic landscapes. Nature Communications, 2016.  
   METABRIC extended molecular profiling study.

## Machine learning and interpretation

9. Pedregosa F, Varoquaux G, Gramfort A, et al. Scikit-learn: Machine Learning in Python. Journal of Machine Learning Research, 2011.  
   Machine-learning library used for Logistic Regression, Random Forest, preprocessing, and evaluation.

10. Lundberg SM and Lee SI. A Unified Approach to Interpreting Model Predictions. Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems, 2017.  
    SHAP explainability reference.

## Pathway enrichment

11. Chen EY, Tan CM, Kou Y, et al. Enrichr: interactive and collaborative HTML5 gene list enrichment analysis tool. BMC Bioinformatics, 2013.  
    Enrichment resource related to gene-list pathway analysis.

12. Kuleshov MV, Jones MR, Rouillard AD, et al. Enrichr: a comprehensive gene set enrichment analysis web server 2016 update. Nucleic Acids Research, 2016.  
    Updated Enrichr reference.

13. Liberzon A, Birger C, Thorvaldsdóttir H, et al. The Molecular Signatures Database Hallmark Gene Set Collection. Cell Systems, 2015.  
    MSigDB Hallmark gene-set reference.

## Notes

This project used publicly available datasets and open-source computational tools. For formal manuscript submission, references should be formatted according to the target journal style.
